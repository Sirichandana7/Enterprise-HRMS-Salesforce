# 1. Introduction

The Enterprise Human Resource Management System (HRMS) is a Salesforce-based enterprise application designed to digitize and automate the complete employee lifecycle. The system centralizes HR operations such as recruitment, employee onboarding, attendance, leave management, performance evaluation, document management, and employee offboarding.

The solution leverages Salesforce Administration, Flow Builder, Approval Processes, Reports, Dashboards, Apex, Lightning Web Components (LWC), and Agentforce to provide a secure, scalable, and intelligent HR platform.

---

# 2. Business Background

ABC Global Technologies Pvt. Ltd. is a multinational IT consulting company with employees working across multiple departments and locations.

Currently, HR activities are managed using spreadsheets, emails, and disconnected applications, resulting in:

- Manual employee record maintenance
- Delayed approval processes
- Duplicate data entry
- Limited reporting capabilities
- Poor visibility into workforce analytics
- Time-consuming recruitment tracking

The organization requires a centralized cloud-based HRMS to improve efficiency, automate business processes, and enhance employee experience.
